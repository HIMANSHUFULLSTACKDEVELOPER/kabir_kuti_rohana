KABIR SAHEB

The years of Kabir's birth and death are uncertain. Some historians favor 1398–1448 as the period Kabir lived, while others favor 1440–1518 Generally, Kabir is believed to have been born in 1398 (Samvat 1455),[on the full moon day of Jyeshtha month (according to the historical Hindu calendar Vikram Samvat) at the time of Brahmamuharta. There is a considerable scholarly debate on the circumstances surrounding Kabir's birth. Many followers of Kabir believe that he came from Satloka by assuming the body of light, and incarnated on a lotus flower and claim that the rishi Ashtanand was the direct witness of this incident, who himself appeared on a lotus flower in the Lahartara Pond.A few accounts mention that Kabir in the form of a child was found at Lahartara Lake by a Muslim weaver called Niru and his wife Nima who raised him as his parents...

Prakashmuni Naam Sahab

Dharamdas ji (from Bandhavgarh, Madhya Pradesh) was a dear disciple of Kabir Sahib. Supreme God Kabir Ji had said to Saint Dharmdas ji that to maintain religiousness, only give first mantra to your son Churamani, by which he will remain religious and your lineage will continue. Then Kabir Sahib said that a messenger of Kaal will come in your seventh generation. He will even finish this actual first mantra and will propagate another arbitrary mantra. The eleventh, thirteenth and the seventeenth Mahants will finish the rest of the religiousness. In this way, bhakti will finish from your lineage, but your lineage will still continue until the forty-second (42) generation. Then your lineage will also get destroyed.

Udit Muni Naam Sahab

Saheb Bandagi Saheb. Bandhavgarh is a place where Sadguru Kabir Saheb told to Dhani Dharamdas Saheb that wandering here and there is not worship of God. The worship of big stautes is not worship of God. A man who leaves his family is not a true saint, as in early days people leave their houses and went to the forest for worship of God. In fact this is a place, where the Sahab Kabir shown/make realised the truth of real God to Dhani Dharamdas Saheb. First time in the history only and only Dhani Dharamdas Sahab urged Sadguru Kabir to Link the soul of mine and my family to the truth, after remembring the sweetness, lightness and knowledge in the kingdom of True God. The holy soul of Dharamdas ji realised the hate and pain in this world and asked to Sadguru to realise all the world the truth of God. Sahab give his words which can not be spoken to Dhani Dharamdas Sahab in the Form of Vansh-42. So, Sadguru Kabir is alive at Damakhera in the form of Vansh-42. The present Vansh Guru, Panthshri Huzur Prakash Mani Naam Saheb is showing the path of devotion which leads to Satyalok, means the country of truth at Damakhera, near Raipur, Chattisgarh.
